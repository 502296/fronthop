Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FrontHop collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details (name, email, phone) when you post jobs or join as a pro.
  • Job/project descriptions and uploaded images.
  • Optional business info from contractors (licenses, insurance, websites).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect homeowners with relevant contractors.
  • To send important updates, job notifications, or alerts.
  • To improve our platform and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information is shared only to match homeowners and pros or when required by law.

4. Security

We use encrypted connections and secure hosting to protect data. No method of transmission is 100% secure, so please use caution when sharing personal info.

5. Cookies

FrontHop may use cookies to remember preferences and improve navigation. You may disable cookies in your browser.

6. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated. We encourage users to review it periodically.

FrontHop is a marketplace connecting homeowners with independent contractors. We do not perform, supervise, or guarantee any work.
