About FrontHop

FrontHop is a modern marketplace that connects homeowners with vetted local pros. Post your project once — receive multiple offers — compare price, reviews, and ETA — hire with confidence.

What we do

We connect, you choose

We don’t perform or supervise the work. We simply match you with the right professional and keep messaging in one place.

Why it works

Multiple offers → better value

Trust & Safety

Verified profiles & photo proof

Pros share licenses/insurance when applicable and upload before/after photos.

FAQ

Is FrontHop a contractor?

No. FrontHop is a marketplace. All jobs are direct agreements between homeowner and contractor.

How do homeowners pay?

Payment is handled directly with the pro. Online options (e.g., cards) will be added later.

How do pros get projects?

Homeowners post a job with photos. Verified pros receive job alerts and submit offers. The homeowner compares and hires.
